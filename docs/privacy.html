Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

1. Overview

SuperPrompt ("we", "our", "the extension") is a Chrome extension that enhances text prompts for AI tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • Prompts & History: All prompts, history, and library data are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. They never leave your device.
  • Activation Codes: When you activate a paid plan, the activation code is verified either locally (HMAC validation) or against our Google Apps Script backend. Only the code itself is transmitted — no personal information.
  • Payment Data: Payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not handle, see, or store your card numbers, UPI IDs, or banking credentials. See Razorpay's Privacy Policy.
  • Email: If you pay via Razorpay Payment Link, Razorpay collects your email to send the activation code. We use this email solely for sending the activation code.

3. Permissions Explained

  • storage: To save your preferences, history, and activation data locally.
  • clipboardWrite/Read: To copy enhanced prompts to your clipboard.
  • tabs: To open AI tool websites and inject your prompt.
  • scripting: To auto-paste prompts into AI tool input fields.
  • host_permissions: Limited to specific AI tool domains for prompt injection.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Razorpay: Payment processing only. Their Privacy Policy
  • Google Apps Script: Code verification only. No personal data stored beyond activation records (email + code).

5. Data Security

All local data is stored using Chrome's encrypted storage API. Activation codes use HMAC-SHA256 cryptographic verification.

6. Your Rights

You can delete all extension data at any time by going to chrome://extensions → SuperPrompt → Clear data, or by uninstalling the extension.

7. Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
